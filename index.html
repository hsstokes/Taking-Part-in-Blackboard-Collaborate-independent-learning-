Independent Learning in Digital Studies 🌍

Why We Practise This

Independent learning builds creative thinking, confidence, and real studio‑world skills. It helps you work as a designer who can test ideas, solve problems, and make decisions.

How to Work Independently

  • 🖱️ Try first, ask second. Check the guide or video before requesting help.
  • 💬 Use private chat if you want to ask quietly — that’s always welcome.
  • 🖥️ Share your screen if you need technical support; it speeds up help time.
  • 🤝 Support a classmate. Sharing what you know helps both of you learn.
Remember: The best learning happens when you explore, experiment, and try new approaches. Making mistakes is part of the creative process.
If something doesn’t work: Write a short message in chat such as “Need help with brush tool” or “My layers aren’t visible.” I’ll come to you when I can.

How to Take Part in Blackboard Collaborate 💬

1️⃣ Check Your Audio and Microphone

  • Before class starts, click the purple panel (bottom‑right) → open the Settings ⚙️ icon.
  • Choose Set up your camera and microphone.
  • Speak a few words — you should see the microphone bar moving.
  • If you see no movement, check that no other app (like Zoom or Teams) is using your mic.
🎧 Tip: Headphones reduce echo and make your voice clearer.

2️⃣ Using the Chat

  • Click the purple panel → Chat 💬.
  • Type your message into the text box and press Enter.
  • You can write to: • Everyone — the whole group. • Moderator Only — for private help.
✉️ Private messages are especially useful if you are shy or stuck — write “Need help with [tool]”.

3️⃣ Sharing Your Screen

  • Click the Share Content 🖥️ button (two arrows icon) on the panel.
  • Select Share Application / Screen.
  • Choose your entire screen, a window, or just one browser tab.
  • Press Share — a purple border will show around your shared screen.
⚠️ Don’t open personal files while sharing. When you’re finished, press Stop Sharing.

4️⃣ If Something Isn’t Working

  • Use the chat and write a short message like: “Audio not working” or “Can’t see screen share.”
  • Try refreshing the page (Ctrl + R / Cmd + R).
  • Re‑join the session if needed — your place will be saved.
